Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: October 20, 2025

Overview

StayFinder Silk facilitates bookings for short-term stays. Cancellation and refund terms depend on the listing and host. The terms below apply when no host-specific policy is shown.

Standard Cancellation Rules

  • Full refund: Cancellations made at least 7 full days prior to check-in will receive a full refund (excluding any payment gateway fees).
  • Partial refund: Cancellations between 48 hours and 7 days prior to check-in will receive a 50% refund of the booking amount.
  • No refund: Cancellations made within 48 hours of scheduled check-in are non-refundable.

Exceptional Circumstances

For force majeure (natural disaster, government restrictions), refunds are considered case-by-case. Contact us at support@stayfinder.example.

Host-specific Policies

If a listing displays host-specific cancellation policy, that policy overrides the standard terms.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email support@stayfinder.example with your booking ID and reason.
  2. We will acknowledge within 48 hours; additional docs may be requested.

Payment Gateway Fees

Payment gateway transaction fees may not be refundable. We will notify you if fees are non-refundable.
